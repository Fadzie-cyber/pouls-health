### Breaking the Silence on Campus
Our mission at Pouls isn't just digital; it's physical. In late March, our team conducted a dual-campus campaign at the **University of Zimbabwe** and **NUST**. 

We focused on:
* **The First Responder Network:** Training student leaders to recognize medical emergencies.
* **On-Boarding:** Helping students navigate our anonymous dashboard for the first time.

The data is clear: students are desperate for a system they can trust. We are building it.
